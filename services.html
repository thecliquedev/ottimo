SERVICES

At Ottimo Metals, we are proud to be experts in manufacturing high-quality zinc wire and providing specialized services such as corrosion prevention services. Our primary focus is on harnessing the potential of zinc wiring to cater to various industries and projects.

Here are the services we offer:

Turnkey Projects

We undertake comprehensive corrosion protection projects for a wide range of applications, including structural steel, pipes, castings, steel fabrications, cylinders, and other cast/steel products. Our expertise ensures thorough protection and extended durability for your valuable assets.

Consultancy

Our experienced team offers detailed studies and expert consultation to provide customized solutions tailored to your requirements. We analyze your products and provide professional recommendations to effectively build structures, prevent corrosion and extend the lifespan of various constructions.

Technical Support

We believe in delivering complete customer satisfaction. Therefore, we offer comprehensive technical support for the products we manufacture. Our knowledgeable team is ready to assist you with any inquiries, ensuring you receive the best guidance and support for your zinc wiring needs.

In-House Metallizing Facility

Equipped with a state-of-the-art six-axis robotic system, we accurately apply coatings on components of any size. Our facility enables us to work with a wide range of coatings, including metals, alloys, complex carbides, and ceramics, ensuring optimal performance and protection.
Experience the transformative impact of our zinc wiring solutions, providing long-lasting results and reliable protection for your valuable assets. Trust Ottimo Metals as your reliable partner in unlocking the benefits of zinc wiring for your specific industry needs.
