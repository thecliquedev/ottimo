ABOUT US

At Ottimo Metals, we're driven by our passion to provide exceptional solutions for zinc wiring needs. Our zinc wires are crafted with precision and offer long-lasting durability, making them the preferred choice across diverse industries.

Our commitment to excellence is evident in our investment in cutting-edge machinery, ensuring our products consistently meet the highest standards of quality and performance.

As a trusted partner in the zinc wiring industry, we've built a strong reputation based on technical expertise, attentiveness to client needs, and an unwavering commitment to innovation and continuous improvement.

We have imported our completely integrated manufacturing unit and state-of-the-art technology from Italy and set up in West Bengal, India. We have established ourselves as one of the fastest growing zinc exporters known for fulfilling the evolving customer requirements with ease.

MISSION

With state-of-the-art technology integrated into our production system at every stage, our mission at Ottimo Metals is to consistently deliver certified quality products that exceed our clients' current and future requirements. We globally deliver our products that conform to various international standards and specifications for multifold industrial growth.

VISION

At Ottimo Metals, our vision is to establish ourselves as the leading manufacturer and exporter of zinc wires in India and to boost India's industrial growth. We are committed to continually enhancing our production systems, aiming for minimal energy consumption and a zero-waste production cycle.

OUR CORE VALUES

QUALITY

At Ottimo Metals, quality assurance is our primary focus. Through continuous investment in R&D and advanced technology, we ensure the production of high-quality products that conform to various national and international standards. We perform stringent quality check techniques before approval for delivery to customers, earning us a strong reputation both nationally and internationally.

With a completely integrated plant, we prioritize sustainable operations at every step.

At Ottimo, we're committed to upholding a set of core values that guide everything we do.
